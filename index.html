بینظیر انکم سپورٹ پروگرام: BISP ka maksad ghareeb aur mustahiq gharano ko maali madad dena hai. PMT (Proxy Means Test) aik formula hai jo aapki ghar ki maashi surat-e-haal ka andaza lagata hai.

Agar aapka PMT score **30 se kam** hai to aapko **"Ahal" (eligible)** samjha jata hai. Zyada score hone par **"Ghair Ahal"** likha ayega.
Yeh tool sirf aapko idea dene ke liye hai. Official natija sirf BISP ki taraf se diya jata hai.
